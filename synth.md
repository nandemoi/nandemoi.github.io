#### Catesian Product

- an equivalent relation:  
  ||||
  |:--|:--|:--|
  |reflexive|自反|$(x. x) ∈ R$|
  |symmetric|對稱|$(x, y) ∈ R ⇒ (y, x) ∈ R$|
  |transitive|遞移|$(x, y) ∈ R$ and $(y, z) ∈ R ⇒ (x, z) ∈ R$|

- a partial order relation:  
  ||||
  |:--|:--|:--|
  |reflexive|自反|$(x. x) ∈ R$|
  |anti-symmetric|反對稱|$(x, y) ∈ R and $(y, x) ∈ R  ⇒ x = y$|
  |transitive|遞移|$(x, y) ∈ R$ and $(y, z) ∈ R ⇒ (x, z) ∈ R$|
